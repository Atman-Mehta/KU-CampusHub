Scholarships

Empowering Students with Opportunities

Karnavati University believes in nurturing talent and providing equal opportunities for all students. Our scholarship programs are designed to reward academic excellence, encourage extracurricular achievements, and support students with financial needs.

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students with exceptional academic performance.
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: Designed to support students from economically weaker sections.
  • Sports Excellence Scholarships: Recognizing outstanding achievements in sports.
  • Special Scholarships: Provided to students based on specific criteria, including diversity and inclusivity.
