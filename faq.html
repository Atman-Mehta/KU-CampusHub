Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What programs does Karnavati University offer?

Karnavati University offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields such as design, law, management, dentistry, technology, and mass communication.

Where is Karnavati University located?

Karnavati University is located in Uvarsad, Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India.

What is the admission process for undergraduate programs?

Admission to undergraduate programs typically involves eligibility criteria such as completing 10+2 education with required scores, followed by an entrance test or personal interview depending on the program.

Campus Life

What facilities are available on campus?

The campus offers state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, libraries, sports complexes, hostels, cafeterias, and spaces for extracurricular activities.

Are there any extracurricular activities for students?

Yes, students can participate in various extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural events, technical fests, and clubs related to their interests.

Scholarships and Placements

Does Karnavati University offer scholarships?

Yes, the university provides a range of scholarships based on academic merit, financial need, and achievements in sports or other areas.

What are the placement opportunities at Karnavati University?

The university has a dedicated placement cell that collaborates with top recruiters to provide students with excellent internship and placement opportunities across industries.

What companies visit the campus for placements?

Top companies from diverse industries such as IT, design, law, healthcare, and media regularly recruit from Karnavati University. Specific details can be found on the placements page.
