Our Story

As part of the Karnavati University community, we are proud to share our story. Established in 2017 in Uvarsad, near Gandhinagar, Gujarat, we embarked on a mission to offer high-quality education across various disciplines.

Our university was founded under The Gujarat Private Universities (Amendment) Act, 2017, by the Karnavati Medical and Educational Trust, marking the beginning of our journey towards academic excellence and innovation.

At Karnavati University, we are united in our mission to foster an environment of learning, innovation, and success, preparing our students to be future leaders and changemakers in their respective fields.
